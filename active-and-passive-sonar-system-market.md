<p><p>Active and Passive Sonar System Market types are categorized into Low Frequency, Medium Frequency, and High Frequency based on the frequency range used for underwater acoustic signals. Low Frequency sonar systems typically operate below 1 kHz, Medium Frequency systems operate between 1 kHz and 10 kHz, and High Frequency systems operate above 10 kHz. Each frequency range offers unique advantages and drawbacks, with Low Frequency systems providing longer range detection but lower resolution, while High Frequency systems offer higher resolution but shorter range detection capabilities.</p></p>
