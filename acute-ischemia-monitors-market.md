<p><p>Acute ischemia monitors are divided into two main types: benchtop and portable. On the other hand, portable monitors are smaller, lightweight devices that allow for easy transport and monitoring of patients in various clinical settings. Both types of monitors are essential for early detection of ischemic events and prompt intervention to prevent serious complications.</p></p>
