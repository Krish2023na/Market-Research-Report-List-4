<p><p>Adiabatic humidification systems are utilized for increasing humidity levels in indoor environments. There are four main types of systems available in the market: ultrasonic type, high pressure atomizing type, air/water atomizing type, and wetted media evaporative type. Ultrasonic systems use vibrations to create a fine mist, high pressure atomizing systems use pumps to generate a mist, air/water atomizing systems mix air and water to produce moisture, and wetted media evaporative systems rely on evaporation from a wetted medium to humidify the air.</p></p>
