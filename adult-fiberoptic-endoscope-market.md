<p><p>Adult fiberoptic endoscope market is divided into two main types: electronic imaging and optical imaging. Electronic imaging utilizes digital technology to transmit high-quality images of the inside of the body to a monitor for examination. On the other hand, optical imaging uses traditional fiberoptic technology to visualize the internal organs directly through a tiny camera at the end of the endoscope. Both types offer clear and detailed views of the body's interior, helping medical professionals in diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
