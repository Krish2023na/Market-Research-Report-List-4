<p><p>Air Traffic Control Radar (ATC-Radar) Market Types include Primary Surveillance Radar and Secondary Surveillance Radar. Primary Surveillance Radar uses radio waves to detect the presence and position of aircraft, but does not provide identifying information. On the other hand, Secondary Surveillance Radar not only detects aircraft but also retrieves and displays additional data such as aircraft identification, altitude, and speed. These two types of radars play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient air traffic control operations.</p></p>
