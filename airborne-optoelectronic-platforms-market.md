<p><p>Multispectral airborne optoelectronic platforms refer to systems that capture data across multiple discrete bands of the electromagnetic spectrum, providing information on various types of targets. Hyperspectral platforms, on the other hand, capture data across hundreds of contiguous bands, allowing for more detailed and specific analysis of targets. Multispectral systems are ideal for general surveillance and monitoring, while hyperspectral systems are used for more precise target identification and classification in applications such as environmental monitoring, agriculture, and defense.</p></p>
