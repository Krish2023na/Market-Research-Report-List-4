<p><p>Aircraft Helmet Mounted Displays (HMD) market includes two main types of display systems: Monocular Display System and Bi-ocular Display System. The Monocular Display System consists of a single display screen in front of one eye, providing key flight information and targeting data to the pilot. On the other hand, the Bi-ocular Display System features two display screens in front of both eyes, offering a wider field of view and more immersive experience for the pilot. Both types of display systems are crucial in enhancing situational awareness and improving operational efficiency in military and commercial aviation.</p></p>
