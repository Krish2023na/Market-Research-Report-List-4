<p><p>Airship market types refer to the different structures of airships based on their rigidity. Rigid airships have a framework that maintains the shape of the ship even when deflated. Semi-rigid airships have a partial framework that provides some shape stability. Non-rigid airships do not have a rigid framework and rely on internal pressure to maintain their shape. Each type offers advantages and disadvantages in terms of cost, weight, and flexibility, catering to different needs in industries such as transportation, advertising, and surveillance.</p></p>
