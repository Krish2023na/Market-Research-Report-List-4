<p><p>Ammonia absorption towers are commonly used in industries for removing ammonia gas from various processes. Two main types of ammonia absorption towers are plate towers and spray towers. Plate towers utilize a series of plates to promote contact between the ammonia gas and the absorption liquid, while spray towers use a spray nozzle to disperse the absorption liquid into droplets for efficient gas-liquid contact. Both types of towers have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
