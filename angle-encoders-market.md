<p><strong>The Angle Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Encoders</li><li>Incremental Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Angle encoders are devices used to measure the angular position of a rotating shaft. Absolute encoders provide a unique digital code for each position of the shaft, ensuring accuracy even after power loss. Incremental encoders generate a series of pulses as the shaft rotates, allowing for relative position measurement. The market for absolute encoders is driven by industries requiring high precision such as robotics and automotive, while incremental encoders are common in applications where cost and space are constraints, like consumer electronics.</p></p>
