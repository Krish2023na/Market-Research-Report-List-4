<p><p>Anhydrous substance diatomite is available in three main market types: natural, calcined, and flux calcined. Natural diatomite is the raw unaltered form, while calcined diatomite is heated to high temperatures to remove organic material. Flux calcined diatomite undergoes an additional step where it is mixed with fluxing agents to enhance its properties. Each market type has unique characteristics and applications, catering to different industries such as filtration, agriculture, construction, and more.</p></p>
