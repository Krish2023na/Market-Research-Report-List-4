<p><p>Anti-peptic ulcer drugs market offers different types of medications to treat peptic ulcers and related conditions. Proton pump inhibitors (PPIs) reduce stomach acid production, while Potassium-Competitive Acid Blockers (P-CAB) work by targeting specific cellular mechanisms. Antacids neutralize stomach acid, H2 antagonists block histamine receptors to reduce acid production, and ulcer protective drugs help protect the stomach lining. Each type of medication targets different aspects of ulcer treatment, providing a range of options for patients with peptic ulcer disease.</p></p>
