<p><p>Applicant Tracking System (ATS) software is available in two main types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise ATS software is installed locally on a company's servers and managed by their IT department. This type is known for providing more control and customization options but can be more costly and time-consuming to maintain. On the other hand, Cloud-based ATS software is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through a web browser. This type is more cost-effective, easily scalable, and offers remote access from anywhere with an internet connection.</p></p>
