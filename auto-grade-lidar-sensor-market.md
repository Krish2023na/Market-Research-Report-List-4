<p><p>Auto Grade LiDAR sensors are commonly categorized into two main types: Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. </p><p>Mechanical LiDAR sensors use rotating mirrors to scan the environment and generate a 3D map, while Solid State LiDAR sensors rely on a stationary array of laser emitters to achieve the same task without any moving parts. </p><p>Both technologies have their own advantages and limitations, with Mechanical LiDAR typically offering longer range and higher resolution, while Solid State LiDAR is more compact, reliable, and durable.</p></p>
