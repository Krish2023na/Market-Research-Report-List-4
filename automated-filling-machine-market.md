<p><p>Automated filling machines can be categorized into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic filling machines operate independently once programmed, requiring minimal human intervention. They are ideal for high-volume production lines where efficiency and precision are crucial. On the other hand, semi-automatic filling machines require some manual assistance, such as placing containers or activating the filling process. They are often used in smaller production facilities or for products that require more customization or delicate handling.</p></p>
