<p><p>Flow Cell Based System and Direct Read System are two types of Automatic Discrete Chemical Analyzers used in laboratory settings. Flow Cell Based Systems utilize a continuous flow of samples through a cell for analysis, allowing for high-throughput and rapid results. Direct Read Systems, on the other hand, provide instantaneous readings of samples without the need for continuous flow. Both systems offer efficient and accurate analysis of discrete chemical components in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, environmental, and food testing.</p></p>
