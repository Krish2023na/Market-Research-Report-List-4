<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Fully-Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic lipstick filling machines can be categorized into two main types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention, such as placing the lipstick tubes or removing them once filled. Fully-automatic machines, on the other hand, operate without any manual input, from filling the lipstick to capping and labeling the tubes. Both types cater to different production needs and budgets, with fully-automatic machines being more efficient and suitable for high-volume production lines.</p></p>
