<p><p>An Automatic Plasma Expressor Machine comes in two market types: Single Press and Double Press. Single Press machines have one pressing unit, making them more suitable for smaller laboratories or clinics with lower demand for plasma extraction. Double Press machines, on the other hand, have two pressing units, allowing for faster and more efficient plasma extraction, making them ideal for larger hospitals or laboratories with higher demand. Both types offer automated operation, speeding up the process and reducing the risk of human error in plasma extraction.</p></p>
