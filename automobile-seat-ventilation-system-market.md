<p><strong>The Automobile Seat Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blow</li><li>Suction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile seat ventilation systems come in two types - blow and suction. In the blow system, air is blown through the seat cushion and backrest, providing a cooling effect to the driver or passengers. On the other hand, the suction system works by extracting hot air from the seat, helping to keep the occupants comfortable. Both systems aim to regulate the temperature of the seats in the car, enhancing overall comfort during long drives and hot weather conditions.</p></p>
