<p><ul><li>Three-way Catalysts (TWC)</li><li>Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC)</li><li>Lean NOx Trap (LNT)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)</li><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Catalytic Converter Devices are essential components in vehicles to reduce harmful emissions released into the atmosphere. Three-way Catalysts (TWC) are commonly used in gasoline engines to control emissions of carbon monoxide, hydrocarbons, and nitrogen oxides. Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC) and Lean NOx Trap (LNT) are used in diesel engines to reduce nitrogen oxide emissions. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) technology is commonly used in diesel vehicles to convert nitrogen oxides into harmless nitrogen and water. Diesel Oxidation Catalysts (DOC) helps to reduce emissions of carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons from diesel engines.</p></p>
