<p><p>The automotive intake air filter market can be divided into three main types: paper intake air filters, gauze intake air filters, and foam intake air filters. Paper intake air filters are made from a pleated paper material and are commonly used in most vehicles due to their efficiency in capturing particles. Gauze intake air filters are made from a stainless steel mesh and are known for their durability and superior airflow. Foam intake air filters are made from a sponge-like material and offer excellent filtration capabilities for high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
