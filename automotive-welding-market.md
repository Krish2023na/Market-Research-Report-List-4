<p><strong>The Automotive Welding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inert Protect Type</li><li>Semi-Inert Gas Protect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive welding market includes two main types of welding techniques - Inert Protect Type and Semi-Inert Gas Protect Type. Inert Protect Type involves using gases such as argon, helium, or a mixture of these gases to shield the welding area from atmospheric contamination, creating a cleaner weld. On the other hand, Semi-Inert Gas Protect Type uses a combination of inert and reactive gases like carbon dioxide to produce a strong weld joint. Both techniques are widely used in the automotive industry for various welding applications.</p></p>
