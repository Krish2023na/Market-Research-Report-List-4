<p><p>The Autonomous Underground Mining Equipment market includes various types such as Venting, Hard Rock Equipment, Longwall Systems, Room & Pillar, Entry Development, Tunneling Equipment, and Crushing & Conveying. Venting equipment is used for ventilation in underground mines, while Hard Rock Equipment is specifically designed for mining in hard rock conditions. Longwall Systems involve a method of extracting coal using a long wall of coal in a single slice. Room & Pillar mining involves leaving behind pillars of ore to support the roof. Entry Development equipment is used to create access points in underground mines, while Tunneling Equipment is used for excavating tunnels. Crushing & Conveying equipment involves crushing the ore and transporting it to the surface.</p></p>
