<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ballast ICs are essential components in lighting circuits, ensuring stable operation of fluorescent lamps. They are available in two main market types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount Ballast ICs are compact and suited for high-density circuit board applications. They are soldered directly onto the surface of the board. Through Hole Ballast ICs, on the other hand, are larger and designed for traditional circuit board mounting techniques, with pins inserted through holes on the board and soldered in place. Both types are widely used in lighting systems.</p></p>
