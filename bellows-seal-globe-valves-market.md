<p><p>Bellows seal globe valves are available in two main types: flange type and socket weld type. Flange type bellows seal globe valves are designed with flanges at the ends of the valve body, allowing for connection to pipes through bolting. On the other hand, socket weld type bellows seal globe valves have sockets welded directly onto the valve body, enabling easy connection to pipes through welding. Both types provide efficient and leak-proof sealing solutions for various industrial applications.</p></p>
