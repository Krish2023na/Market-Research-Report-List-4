<p><ul><li>Mechanical Briquetting Machines</li><li>Screw Briquetting Machines</li><li>Hydraulic Briquetting Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Briquetting machines are used to compress biomass materials into dense and solid fuel blocks. There are three main types of briquetting machines in the market: mechanical, screw, and hydraulic. Mechanical briquetting machines use a mechanical press to compress the biomass material, while screw briquetting machines use a screw to push the material forward. Hydraulic briquetting machines use hydraulic pressure to compress the material. Each type of machine offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
