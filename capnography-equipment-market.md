<p><strong>The Capnography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mainstream Capnographys</li><li>Sidestream Capnographys</li><li>Microstream Capnographys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capnography equipment market is segmented into three types - Mainstream Capnography, Sidestream Capnography, and Microstream Capnography. </p><p>Mainstream Capnography devices directly measure the carbon dioxide levels in the patient's exhaled breath. Sidestream Capnography devices use a sample tube to draw a small amount of exhaled gas and analyze it. Microstream Capnography devices are the most advanced, using a microsensor to provide continuous and accurate monitoring of CO2 levels.</p><p>Each type of capnography equipment has its own advantages and applications in various medical settings.</p></p>
