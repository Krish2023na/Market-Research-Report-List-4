<p><p>Central line catheters come in various market types including single-lumen, double-lumen, triple-lumen, and other specialized types. Single-lumen catheters have one lumen for medication administration, while double-lumen catheters have two lumens for medication and blood withdrawal. Triple-lumen catheters have an additional lumen for blood sampling or administration of additional medications. Other specialized central line catheters may have different lumens for specific purposes like hemodialysis or chemotherapy. Each type of catheter serves different medical needs and is chosen based on the patient's condition and treatment requirements.</p></p>
