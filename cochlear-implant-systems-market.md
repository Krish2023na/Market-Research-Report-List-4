<p><ul><li>Unilateral</li><li>Binaural</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A unilateral cochlear implant system involves the use of only one implant in one ear, while a binaural system involves the use of two implants, one in each ear. Unilateral systems are typically used by individuals with severe to profound hearing loss in one ear, while binaural systems are commonly used by those with bilateral hearing loss or who experience difficulty hearing in noisy environments. The choice between unilateral and binaural systems depends on the individual's specific hearing needs and preferences.</p></p>
