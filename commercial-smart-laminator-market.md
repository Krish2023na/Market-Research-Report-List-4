<p><p>The commercial smart laminator market includes two main types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Fully automatic laminators, on the other hand, operate entirely on their own once the materials are loaded, without any need for manual input. Both types cater to different customer needs based on their desired level of automation and convenience in the laminating process.</p></p>
