<p><ul><li>Pyrotechnic Inflator</li><li>Stored Gas Inflator</li><li>Hybrid Inflator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial vehicle airbag inflators are categorized into three main types: pyrotechnic inflator, stored gas inflator, and hybrid inflator. Pyrotechnic inflators use a chemical reaction to rapidly inflate the airbag in the event of a collision. Stored gas inflators release compressed gas to inflate the airbag. Hybrid inflators combine elements of both pyrotechnic and stored gas inflators for efficient and reliable airbag deployment. Each type of inflator has its unique benefits and applications in the commercial vehicle industry.</p></p>
