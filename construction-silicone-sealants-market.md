<p><ul><li>Acetoxy</li><li>Oxime</li><li>Alkoxy</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Construction silicone sealants market can be classified into different types based on the curing mechanism. Acetoxy sealants release acetic acid as they cure, which gives off a strong vinegar-like smell. Oxime sealants are neutral-curing and do not release any corrosive by-products. Alkoxy sealants are also neutral-curing and release alcohol as they cure. Other types of sealants in the market include oxime, alkoxy, and others that have specific applications and properties based on their chemical compositions and curing mechanisms.</p></p>
