<p><p>Continuous Feed Printers are categorized into two main types - Dot Matrix Printer and Laser Printer market. Dot Matrix Printers use a matrix of pins to create text and images on paper, making them suitable for printing documents with a carbon copy. On the other hand, Laser Printers use a laser beam to transfer text and images onto paper, providing high-quality and fast printing. Both types of printers are widely used in businesses and industries that require continuous printing of large volumes of documents.</p></p>
