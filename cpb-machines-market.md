<p><p>CPB (Cardiopulmonary Bypass) Machines market includes two main types - Single Roller Pump and Double Roller Pump. Single Roller Pump systems utilize one roller to pump blood during heart surgery, while Double Roller Pump systems incorporate two rollers for added efficiency. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining blood circulation and oxygenation during cardiopulmonary bypass procedures, with Double Roller Pump systems typically offering higher flow rates and improved control. Healthcare facilities choose between these options based on specific surgical requirements and patient needs.</p></p>
