<p><ul><li>CIC</li><li>ITC</li><li>ITE</li><li>BTE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CIC (Completely in the Canal) hearing aids are the smallest type, fitting entirely inside the ear canal. ITC (In the Canal) aids are slightly larger and partly visible in the ear. ITE (In the Ear) aids are custom-made to fit in the outer ear. BTE (Behind the Ear) aids are worn behind the ear with a tube connecting to a custom earpiece. Each market type caters to different levels of hearing loss, preferences, and cosmetic concerns.</p></p>
