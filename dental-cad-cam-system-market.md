<p><p>Dental CAD & CAM System stands for Computer-Aided Design and Computer-Aided Manufacturing systems used in dentistry. These systems are used to create dental restorations like crowns, bridges, veneers, and dentures with high precision and accuracy. CAD involves creating a digital 3D model of the restoration, while CAM involves the automated milling of the restoration from a block of ceramic or composite material.</p><p>The Dental CAD & CAM System Market is witnessing significant growth due to factors such as the increasing prevalence of dental disorders, technological advancements in the dental industry, rising demand for cosmetic dentistry, and the growing adoption of digital dentistry practices. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Dental CAD & CAM System market is the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms into CAD/CAM software, which helps in automating certain processes and improving the precision of dental restorations. Additionally, the increasing adoption of intraoral scanners and 3D printing technologies is further driving the growth of the market. The demand for same-day dental restorations and personalized dental solutions is also contributing to the expansion of the Dental CAD & CAM System market.</p></p>
