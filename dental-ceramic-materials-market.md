<p><ul><li>Opaque</li><li>Translucent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental ceramic materials come in various types, with opaque and translucent being the main categories. Opaque ceramics are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for dental crowns and bridges that require more structural support. On the other hand, translucent ceramics are renowned for their lifelike appearance and ability to mimic natural tooth color and transparency. These are commonly used for veneers and inlays where aesthetics are a top priority. Both types cater to different needs in the dental industry, providing dentists with versatile options for various treatments.</p></p>
