<p><p>Diffractive beam splitters are used in a variety of markets, including beam shaping (or top-hat), beam splitting, and beam foci. In beam shaping, diffractive beam splitters help to shape laser beams into uniform intensity profiles, which is important for applications like material processing. Beam splitting involves dividing a single laser beam into multiple beams, useful in microscopy or telecommunications. Beam foci refer to the ability of diffractive beam splitters to concentrate light at specific focal points, ideal for applications like laser surgery or laser printing.</p></p>
