<p><p>A diffusion furnace is a type of furnace used in the semiconductor industry to carry out the process of diffusion in the fabrication of silicon wafers. Diffusion is a key process used to introduce impurities into the crystal lattice structure of silicon in order to alter its electrical properties. The diffusion furnace consists of a tube-shaped chamber where the silicon wafers are heated to high temperatures and exposed to dopant gases, allowing the impurities to diffuse into the silicon.</p><p>The diffusion furnace market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.3% during the forecast period. <p><p>Diffusion Furnace Market includes two main types - Horizontal Tube Reactor and Vertical Tube Reactor. Horizontal Tube Reactor features a horizontal orientation with multiple processing tubes arranged side by side. It is suitable for high-volume production due to its efficient use of space. Vertical Tube Reactor, on the other hand, has a vertical arrangement of processing tubes, making it more suitable for smaller-scale production or research purposes. Both types offer controlled heating environments for various diffusion processes in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
