<p><p>Digital Fabrication Inkjet Inks can be classified into two main types based on the type of colorants used - Dye and Pigment. Dye-based inks are known for their vibrant colors and are often used for high-quality photo printing. On the other hand, pigment-based inks are more durable and are preferred for applications where longevity and resistance to fading are essential, such as outdoor signage. Both types of inks have their own advantages and are widely used in the digital fabrication industry.</p></p>
