<p><p>Digital Microsensor market can be categorized into three main types - Chemical Microsensors, Biological Microsensors, and Physical Microsensors. Chemical microsensors detect and measure chemical properties in gases or liquids. Biological microsensors are used to detect biological molecules or cells, often for medical or environmental monitoring purposes. Physical microsensors measure physical quantities such as pressure, temperature, or motion. Each type of microsensor plays a crucial role in various industries such as healthcare, environmental monitoring, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
