<p><p>Disposable respirators are personal protective equipment designed to protect individuals from inhaling hazardous substances such as dust, fumes, and pathogens. There are two main types of disposable respirators: valved and unvalved. Valved disposable respirators have a one-way valve that allows for easy exhalation, reducing heat and moisture buildup inside the mask. Unvalved disposable respirators do not have a valve, providing a more compact design. Both types offer protection against airborne particles, with valved respirators providing better comfort for prolonged use.</p></p>
