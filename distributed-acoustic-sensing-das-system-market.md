<p><p>Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS) System market includes Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing and Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing. Longitudinal Wave Acoustic Sensing utilizes the propagation of sound waves parallel to the direction of the wave motion, providing accurate measurements of pressure and temperature. On the other hand, Transverse Wave Acoustic Sensing involves the detection of sound waves that move perpendicular to the direction of the wave motion, enabling the monitoring of strain and vibration in various applications. Both types offer unique capabilities for different sensing requirements in the market.</p></p>
