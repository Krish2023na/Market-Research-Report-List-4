<p><p>Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) are wireless communication systems that enhance cellular network coverage. Active DAS utilizes signal boosters to distribute coverage, Passive DAS employs cabling and antennas to amplify signals, and Hybrid DAS combines elements of both systems for optimal coverage. Active DAS is suitable for large, high-traffic areas like stadiums and airports, Passive DAS is cost-effective for small to medium-sized buildings, and Hybrid DAS offers a versatile solution for a variety of environments. These market types cater to different needs for enhanced connectivity.</p></p>
