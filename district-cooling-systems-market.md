<p><strong>The District Cooling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free Cooling</li><li>Absorption Cooling</li><li>Electric Chillers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>District cooling systems offer various market types to meet the cooling needs of different industries and commercial buildings. Free cooling systems use ambient air or water to cool the system without the need for mechanical refrigeration. Absorption cooling systems utilize a solution of water and lithium bromide to provide cooling without traditional electric chillers. Electric chillers use electricity to cool the system by compressing refrigerant gases. Each market type offers unique benefits in terms of energy efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and environmental impact.</p></p>
