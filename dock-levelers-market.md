<p><p>The dock levelers market consists of three main types: mechanical, hydraulic, and others. Mechanical dock levelers operate using springs and hold-down mechanisms, offering a cost-effective solution for light to medium-duty applications. Hydraulic dock levelers use hydraulic systems to raise and lower the platform, providing a more efficient and durable option for heavy-duty applications. The "others" category includes air-powered and vertical dock levelers, offering unique solutions for specific loading dock requirements. Each type of dock leveler caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
