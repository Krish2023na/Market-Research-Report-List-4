<p><p>The Electric Oil Pump market can be divided into two main types: Integrated Pump and Separate Pump. Integrated Pump refers to a system where the oil pump is designed as a part of the engine assembly, providing lubrication and cooling functions seamlessly. On the other hand, Separate Pump involves a standalone pump that operates independently from the engine, allowing for more flexibility and customization in oil circulation. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
