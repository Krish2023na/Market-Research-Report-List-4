<p><p>Electric wheelchairs are available in two main market types: dry-battery electric wheelchairs and wet-battery electric wheelchairs. Dry-battery electric wheelchairs use sealed lead-acid batteries that are maintenance-free, lightweight, and easy to transport. On the other hand, wet-battery electric wheelchairs use traditional lead-acid batteries that require regular maintenance, are heavier, and may be more difficult to transport. Users can choose between these two types based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
