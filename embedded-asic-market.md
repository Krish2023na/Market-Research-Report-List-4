<p><p>The embedded ASIC market consists of two main types: real-time embedded systems and standalone embedded systems. Real-time embedded systems are designed to perform tasks within a specific time frame, often used in industries such as automotive and aerospace. Both markets cater to different applications and have their unique requirements in terms of performance, reliability, and energy efficiency.</p></p>
