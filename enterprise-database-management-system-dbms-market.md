<p><p>Enterprise Database Management System (DBMS) market can be categorized into two major types: Relational Database and Nonrelational Database. </p><p>Relational databases store data in tables with rows and columns, following a structured format. They are known for their ability to handle complex queries and ensure data integrity.</p><p>Nonrelational databases, on the other hand, store data in various formats like graphs, documents, key-value pairs, or wide-column stores. They are highly scalable and flexible, making them suitable for handling large volumes of unstructured data.</p></p>
