<p><p>Feedthrough ceramic capacitors are used in various electronic applications to filter noise and provide stability in power distribution systems. There are two main types of feedthrough ceramic capacitors: DC feedthrough capacitors, which are designed for direct current applications, and AC feedthrough capacitors, which are optimized for alternating current systems. DC feedthrough capacitors have low impedance and are suitable for voltage regulation, while AC feedthrough capacitors have high capacitance and are ideal for filtering high-frequency noise in AC circuits.</p></p>
