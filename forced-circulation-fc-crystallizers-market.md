<p><p>Forced Circulation (FC) Crystallizers are classified based on circulation type and mounting type. In terms of circulation type, they can be either internal or external. Internal FC Crystallizers have circulation systems within the crystallizer vessel, while External FC Crystallizers have separate circulation loops outside the vessel. In terms of mounting type, FC Crystallizers can be either vertical or horizontal. Vertical FC Crystallizers are mounted upright, while horizontal FC Crystallizers are mounted sideways. Each type of FC Crystallizer has its own set of advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
