<p><p>GNSS SoCs are categorized based on the frequency bands they operate in, including L1 Band, B1 Band, E1 Band, L5 Band, and Others. L1 Band is commonly used for civilian and commercial applications, B1 Band is used for satellite-based augmentation systems, E1 Band is utilized for European GNSS signals, and L5 Band is known for its higher accuracy and integrity. The Others category encompasses various other frequency bands utilized for specific applications within the GNSS market. Each band offers unique features and benefits for different GNSS applications.</p></p>
