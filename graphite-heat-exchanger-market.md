<p><p>Graphite heat exchangers are used in various industries for efficient heat transfer. The market types include shell and tubes, blocks, and plates. Shell and tube heat exchangers are cylindrical in shape with tubes running through them, allowing for high heat transfer rates. Block heat exchangers have a stacked design, providing a large surface area for heat exchange. Plate heat exchangers consist of thin, corrugated plates stacked together, offering a compact and efficient heat transfer solution. Each type caters to specific requirements in different industries.</p></p>
