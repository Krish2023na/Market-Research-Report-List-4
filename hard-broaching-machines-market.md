<p><strong>The Hard Broaching Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Pull</li><li>Spiral Pull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hard broaching machines come in different types such as Straight Pull and Spiral Pull. Straight Pull machines are the most common type, where the broach is pulled straight through the workpiece. Spiral Pull machines, on the other hand, have a helical grooving on the broach, allowing for a smoother and more efficient cutting process. Both types have their own advantages and are used based on the specific requirements of the broaching operation.</p></p>
