<p><p>High Performance Computing Cluster (HPCC) market can be categorized into two main types - on-premises and cloud-based markets. On-premises HPCC involve physical clusters installed and managed within an organization's own data center, providing complete control and security. Cloud-based HPCC, on the other hand, offer scalable and flexible computing resources on a pay-as-you-go basis, allowing organizations to quickly deploy and scale their HPC infrastructure without the need for large capital investments. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of organizations based on factors such as security, control, cost, and scalability.</p></p>
