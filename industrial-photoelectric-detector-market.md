<p><p>The industrial photoelectric detector market is segmented into three main types: photodiode, phototransistor, and others. Photodiodes are semiconductor devices that convert light into current, while phototransistors amplify and detect light signals. The "Others" category includes different types of photoelectric detectors such as photovoltaic cells, photoconductive cells, and photomultiplier tubes. Each type offers unique characteristics and applications in industrial settings for tasks such as object detection, light sensing, and proximity sensing.</p></p>
