<p><strong>The Industrial X-ray Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screen Type Films</li><li>Non-Screen Type Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial X-ray film market is divided into two main types: Screen Type Films and Non-Screen Type Films. Screen Type Films are used with intensifying screens to enhance image quality and sensitivity. They are suitable for high-resolution applications requiring detailed imaging. On the other hand, Non-Screen Type Films do not require intensifying screens and are used for lower resolution applications. They are more cost-effective and easy to use compared to Screen Type Films. Both types of films play a crucial role in industrial X-ray imaging applications.</p></p>
