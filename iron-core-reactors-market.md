<p><p>Iron core reactors are essential components in electrical systems to regulate voltage and control power flow. Two common types of iron core reactors are dry type and oil immersed type. Dry type reactors are enclosed in a sealed chamber and are ideal for indoor use as they do not require any maintenance or refilling of oil. On the other hand, oil immersed reactors are immersed in oil for cooling and insulation purposes and are typically used in outdoor or high-voltage applications. Both types play a critical role in ensuring efficient and stable power distribution.</p></p>
