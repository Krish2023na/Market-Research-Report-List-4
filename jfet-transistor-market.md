<p><p>JFET transistors are categorized into two main types: N-Channel and P-Channel. N-Channel JFET transistors are made using n-type semiconductor material and conduct when a negative voltage is applied to the gate terminal. P-Channel JFET transistors are made using p-type semiconductor material and conduct when a positive voltage is applied to the gate terminal. Both types of JFET transistors are commonly used in electronic circuits for applications such as amplification, switching, and signal processing.</p></p>
