<p><ul><li>Primary (Cemented & Cementless)</li><li>Revision Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Knee reconstruction devices market can be categorized into primary implants, which include cemented and cementless implants, and revision implants. Primary implants are used in initial knee replacement surgeries to replace damaged knee joints with artificial implants. Cemented implants are fixed to the bone with bone cement, while cementless implants rely on bone ingrowth for stability. Revision implants are used in cases where a previous knee replacement has failed and a new implant is required to correct problems or address complications.</p></p>
