<p><strong>The Kraft Lignin Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Softwood Kraft Lignin</li><li>Hardwood Kraft Lignin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kraft lignin products are derived from the pulping process of wood, specifically softwood and hardwood. Softwood kraft lignin is extracted from softwood trees like pine and spruce, while hardwood kraft lignin is derived from hardwood trees such as oak and birch. Softwood kraft lignin is known for its higher purity and lower sulfur content, making it suitable for applications in industries like construction and pharmaceuticals. Hardwood kraft lignin, on the other hand, is preferred for its higher yield and lower cost, making it popular in applications like adhesives and coatings.</p></p>
