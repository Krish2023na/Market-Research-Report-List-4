<p><p>Laparoscopes are medical devices used for minimally invasive surgeries. There are two main types of laparoscopes - rigid and flexible. Rigid laparoscopes have a fixed structure and provide a more stable and clear image during procedures. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different surgical settings based on the specific requirements of the procedure.</p></p>
