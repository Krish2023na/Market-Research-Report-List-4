<p><p>Laser Acousto-Optic Deflectors (AODF) are used in various applications such as laser scanning, material processing, and medical imaging. The two main types of AODF are Free-Space Acousto-Optic Deflectors, which are used for free-space optical systems, and Fiber-Coupled Acousto-Optic Deflectors, which are integrated into fiber-optic systems. Free-Space AODFs provide high-speed laser beam steering capabilities, while Fiber-Coupled AODFs are ideal for applications requiring precise control and manipulation of laser beams within fiber-optic setups.</p></p>
