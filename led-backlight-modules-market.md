<p><strong>The LED Backlight Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Lit RGB LED</li><li>Direct-Lit White LED</li><li>Edge-Lighted LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED backlight modules come in three main types: Direct-Lit RGB LED, Direct-Lit White LED, and Edge-Lighted LED. Direct-Lit RGB LED modules utilize individual red, green, and blue LEDs to produce a wide range of colors. Direct-Lit White LED modules use white LEDs for a consistent, even backlight. Edge-Lighted LED modules have LEDs placed along the edges of the display, which allows for a thinner design and can create dynamic lighting effects. Each type offers unique benefits for different applications.</p></p>
