<p><p>Light Truck Tire (LT) market consists of two main types: Bias Tire and Radial Tire. Bias tires are constructed with crisscrossed layers of ply cords made of fabric, providing durability and strength. These tires have a stiffer ride quality and are more suitable for off-road and heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, Radial tires have plies arranged radially across the tire, providing a smoother ride and better fuel efficiency. They are ideal for on-road driving and offer improved handling and traction.</p></p>
