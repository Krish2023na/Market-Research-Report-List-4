<p><p>Linear Variable Differential Transformer (LVDT) sensors are used to measure linear displacement in a variety of applications. There are two main types of LVDT sensors: DC operated LVDT sensors and AC operated LVDT sensors. </p><p>DC operated LVDT sensors are powered by a direct current source and are typically used in applications where a stable and reliable signal is required. AC operated LVDT sensors, on the other hand, are powered by an alternating current source and are favored for applications where high sensitivity and accuracy are needed. Both types of LVDT sensors offer precise and consistent measurements for various industrial and technological purposes.</p></p>
