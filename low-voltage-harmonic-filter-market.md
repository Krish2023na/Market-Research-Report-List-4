<p><p>Low Voltage Harmonic Filter Market offers two main types of solutions: Active and Passive filters. Active filters use power electronics to actively eliminate harmonics from the electrical system, offering precise and dynamic control over harmonic mitigation. Passive filters, on the other hand, use passive components such as resistors, inductors, and capacitors to reduce harmonics, providing a cost-effective solution for lower harmonic levels. Each type has its own strengths and weaknesses, allowing customers to choose the best solution based on their specific requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
