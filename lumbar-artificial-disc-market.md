<p><p>The lumbar artificial disc market includes two main types: metal and biopolymer. Metal artificial discs are typically made of titanium alloy or stainless steel, offering strong support and durability. On the other hand, biopolymer artificial discs are made of materials like polyethylene or silicone, providing a more flexible and natural feel. Both types aim to replace damaged discs in the lumbar region of the spine, restoring function and reducing pain. The choice between metal and biopolymer discs may depend on factors such as patient preference and surgeon recommendation.</p></p>
