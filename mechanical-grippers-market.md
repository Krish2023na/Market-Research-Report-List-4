<p><p>Mechanical grippers are widely used in industrial automation for gripping and handling objects. There are two main types of mechanical grippers: Electric Grippers and Pneumatic Grippers. Electric Grippers rely on electric motors to open and close the gripper fingers, offering precise control and programmability. On the other hand, Pneumatic Grippers use compressed air to generate gripping force, providing quick and strong gripping action. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific application requirements in the market.</p></p>
