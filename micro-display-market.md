<p><p>Micro-Display market consists of two main types - Reflective Displays and Transmissive Displays. Reflective Displays use ambient light to create images, making them ideal for outdoor use and reducing power consumption. Transmissive Displays require a backlight to display images, providing brighter and more vibrant colors compared to reflective displays. Reflective displays are more energy-efficient but may have lower image quality in low-light conditions, while transmissive displays offer higher image quality but require more power. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
