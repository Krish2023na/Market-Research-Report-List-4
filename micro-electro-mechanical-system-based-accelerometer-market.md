<p><p>Micro Electro Mechanical System (MEMS) based accelerometers are available in various types depending on the number of axes they measure. 1-axis accelerometers measure acceleration along one direction, 2-axis accelerometers measure acceleration along two perpendicular directions, and 3-axis accelerometers measure acceleration along three perpendicular directions. Combo accelerometers combine multiple axes in a single device, providing comprehensive measurement capability. Each type of accelerometer caters to different applications and industries, offering solutions for a wide range of motion sensing requirements.</p></p>
