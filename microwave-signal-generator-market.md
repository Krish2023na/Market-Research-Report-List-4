<p><p>Microwave signal generators are categorized into two main types based on frequency output: low frequency and high frequency. Low frequency signal generators typically operate within the range of a few kilohertz to several megahertz, suitable for applications such as audio testing and low-speed data communication. In contrast, high frequency signal generators operate at frequencies in the gigahertz range, commonly used in applications such as radar systems, wireless communications, and microwave testing. Both low and high frequency signal generators play essential roles in various industries for signal generation and testing purposes.</p></p>
