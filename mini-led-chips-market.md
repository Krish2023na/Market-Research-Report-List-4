<p><strong>The Mini LED Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip LED</li><li>Vertical Chip LED</li><li>Flip Chip LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mini LED chips are available in three main market types: Lateral Chip LED, Vertical Chip LED, and Flip Chip LED. Lateral Chip LEDs feature a layout where the electrical connections are on the same side as the light-emitting surface, allowing for easy assembly. Vertical Chip LEDs have electrical connections on the opposite side of the light-emitting surface, providing better heat dissipation. Flip Chip LEDs have the electrical connections on the bottom of the chip, offering improved thermal management and higher efficiency.</p></p>
