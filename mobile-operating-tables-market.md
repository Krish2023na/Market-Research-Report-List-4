<p><p>Mobile operating tables come in four main types: manual, electric, hydraulic, and electro-hydraulic. Manual tables require physical effort to adjust the height and position of the table. Electric tables use powered motors for easy adjustment. Hydraulic tables operate using hydraulic fluid to control movements. Electro-hydraulic tables combine the benefits of both electric and hydraulic systems for precise and efficient control. Each type provides different levels of convenience and functionality to meet the diverse needs of healthcare facilities.</p></p>
