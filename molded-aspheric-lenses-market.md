<p><strong>The Molded Aspheric Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biconvex</li><li>Convex-concave</li><li>Biconcave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Molded aspheric lenses come in various types, including biconvex, convex-concave, and biconcave. Biconvex lenses have two outwardly curved surfaces, making them ideal for focusing or magnifying images. Convex-concave lenses have one surface that curves inward and one that curves outward, used for light focusing. Biconcave lenses have two inwardly curved surfaces and are commonly used to diverge light. Each type of lens serves a unique purpose in the optics industry, catering to different applications and requirements.</p></p>
