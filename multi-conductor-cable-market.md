<p><ul><li>Shielded</li><li>Unshielded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-Conductor Cable market can be divided into two main types: Shielded and Unshielded. Shielded cables are designed with an outer layer of conductive material to protect signals from external interference, such as electromagnetic interference (EMI) and radio frequency interference (RFI). These cables are commonly used in industrial settings and areas with high levels of interference. On the other hand, Unshielded cables do not have this protective layer, making them more cost-effective but also more susceptible to interference. They are typically used in less demanding environments.</p></p>
