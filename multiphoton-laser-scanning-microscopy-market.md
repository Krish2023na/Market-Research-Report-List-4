<p><p>Multiphoton Laser Scanning Microscopy Market Types include Upright Microscope and Inverted Microscope. Upright Microscopes are designed with the objective lens above the stage, allowing for easier sample manipulation and observation of thicker samples. On the other hand, Inverted Microscopes have the objective lens below the stage, making them ideal for imaging live cells and tissue culture as the samples can be easily maintained on the stage. Both types of microscopes are commonly used in research laboratories and medical institutions for advanced imaging and analysis.</p></p>
