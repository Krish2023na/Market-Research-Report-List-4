<p><strong>The Neurovascular Guidewires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Guide Wire</li><li>Wrapped Guide Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid guide wires are made of a single piece of material, providing excellent torque control for navigating through complex anatomical structures during procedures. On the other hand, wrapped guide wires consist of a core wire wrapped with a secondary material, offering increased flexibility and support. Both types of guide wires are used in neurovascular procedures to navigate through blood vessels and deliver various medical devices such as stents or balloons. The choice between solid and wrapped guide wires depends on the specific requirements of the procedure and the preferences of the healthcare provider.</p></p>
