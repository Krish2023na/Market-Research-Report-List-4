<p><p>Nitrogen Oxide (NOx) Control Equipment in Power Plants includes various types of control technologies such as Low NOx Burners (LNB), Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR), and Selective Non-Catalytic Reduction (SNCR). LNB technology reduces NOx emissions by altering the combustion process, while SCR uses a catalyst to convert NOx into nitrogen and water. SNCR technology involves injecting ammonia or urea into the combustion flue gas to react with NOx. These technologies help power plants comply with emissions regulations and reduce environmental impact.</p></p>
