<p><p>Nonwoven adhesive for personal hygiene products market consists of two main types: hot melt adhesive and water-based adhesives. Hot melt adhesive is a thermoplastic adhesive that is applied in a molten state and solidifies upon cooling, providing high bond strength. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly alternatives that use water as a carrier for adhesive polymers, offering good adhesion properties while being safe for the environment. Both types of adhesives are widely used in the manufacturing of personal hygiene products such as diapers, sanitary napkins, and adult incontinence products.</p></p>
