These nucleotides play crucial roles in cellular processes such as DNA and RNA synthesis. Pyrimidine supplements are commonly used for promoting proper cell growth and repair, while Purine supplements are known for their energy-boosting properties. Both types of nucleotide supplements are essential for maintaining overall health and well-being.</p></p>
