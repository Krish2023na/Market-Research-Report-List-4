<p><p>Offset Printing Press Market is divided into two main types: Sheet-Fed Offset Printing Press and Web-Fed Offset Printing Press. Sheet-Fed Offset presses are suitable for short to medium print runs on various paper sizes, providing high-quality prints with precise color matching. On the other hand, Web-Fed Offset presses are used for high-volume, continuous printing on a continuous roll of paper, making them ideal for newspapers, magazines, and catalogs. Both types play a crucial role in the printing industry, catering to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
