<p><strong>The Oil-trunk Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube Pump</li><li>Rod Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil-trunk pumps can be classified into two main types: Tube pumps and Rod pumps. Tube pumps are designed with a vertical tube to transport fluids to the surface, while Rod pumps utilize a rod to lift fluids from the well. Tube pumps are more commonly used in shallow wells with low viscosity fluids, while Rod pumps are preferred for deeper wells and high viscosity fluids. Both types of pumps play a crucial role in the oil industry by efficiently extracting oil from underground reservoirs.</p></p>
