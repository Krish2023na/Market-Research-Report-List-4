<p><p>Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oils are classified into different market types based on geographical origins and production methods. PGI (Protected Geographical Indication) or IGP (Indicazione Geografica Protetta) oils also have geographical ties but with slightly more flexible production rules. Other markets include oils that may not have geographical indications but still meet organic standards and are produced using high-quality methods.</p></p>
