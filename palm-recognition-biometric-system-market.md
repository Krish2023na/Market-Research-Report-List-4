<p><p>The palm recognition biometric system market comprises two main types of technologies: palmprint and palm vein recognition. Palmprint technology involves scanning the unique patterns on an individual's palm to verify their identity, while palm vein recognition uses near-infrared light to capture the vein patterns beneath the skin's surface for authentication. Both technologies offer high levels of accuracy and security, making them popular choices for applications requiring heightened security measures, such as access control systems and banking services.</p></p>
