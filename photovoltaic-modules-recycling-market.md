<p><p>Photovoltaic Modules recycling market includes different types based on the composition of the modules. Monocrystalline Photovoltaic Modules are made from single crystal silicon, which is known for its high efficiency and durability. Polycrystalline Photovoltaic Modules are made from multiple crystal silicon, providing a cost-effective option with slightly lower efficiency. Thin-film Modules use a variety of materials like cadmium telluride or amorphous silicon, offering flexibility and lightweight design. Each type of module requires specific recycling processes to recover materials efficiently and sustainably.</p></p>
