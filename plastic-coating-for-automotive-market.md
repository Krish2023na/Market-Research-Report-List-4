<p><p>Plastic coating for automotive market consists of three main types: primer, base coat, and clearcoat. Primer is applied first to enhance adhesion and corrosion resistance. Base coat provides color and coverage, while clearcoat gives gloss and protection. These layers work together to provide a durable and attractive finish for automotive parts. Each type plays a specific role in the coating process, with primer laying the foundation, base coat adding color, and clearcoat giving the final shine and protection.</p></p>
