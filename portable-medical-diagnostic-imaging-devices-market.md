<p><p>Portable medical diagnostic imaging devices are instruments that allow healthcare professionals to visualize the internal structures of the body for diagnostic purposes. Common types include CT scanners, X-ray machines, ultrasound devices, and endoscopes. CT scanners use X-rays to create detailed cross-sectional images of the body, X-ray machines emit electromagnetic radiation to produce images of bones and tissues, ultrasound devices use sound waves to generate real-time images of organs, and endoscopes are flexible tubes with cameras used to view the inside of the body.</p></p>
