<p><ul><li>Draw Wire Linear Position Transducers</li><li>Incremental Encoder</li><li>Absolute Position Encoders</li><li>Inductive Proximity Sensors</li><li>LVDT Transducer</li><li>Linear Potentiometer</li><li>Magnetostrictive Transducer</li><li>Non-Contact or Laser Linear Position Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Position transducers are widely used in various industries for measuring linear position. Draw wire linear position transducers provide accurate measurements through a retractable wire. Incremental and absolute encoders use digital signals for precise position detection. Inductive proximity sensors detect the presence of metallic objects without physical contact. LVDT transducers measure displacement with high accuracy. Linear potentiometers provide linear position feedback through a variable resistor. Magnetostrictive transducers use the principle of sound waves to determine position. Non-contact or laser linear position sensors offer contactless measurement for delicate applications.</p></p>
