<p><p>Precut splints are used in medical emergencies to immobilize and support injured body parts. They are available in three main types: head splints, limb splints, and torso splints. Head splints are used to stabilize and protect the head and neck in case of trauma. Limb splints are used to immobilize and support injured arms or legs. Torso splints are used to stabilize the spine and ribs in case of chest or back injuries. Each type serves a specific purpose in providing necessary support and protection to the injured area.</p></p>
