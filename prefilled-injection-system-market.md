<p><p>The prefilled injection system market can be categorized into three main types: glass-based, plastic-based, and others. Glass-based prefilled injection systems are typically used for sensitive drug formulations as they provide higher chemical resistance and protection against contaminants. Plastic-based prefilled injection systems are more commonly used due to their lightweight and break-resistant properties. The "others" category includes alternative materials such as metal or hybrid components. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the drug formulation and delivery method.</p></p>
