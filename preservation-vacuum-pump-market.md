<p><p>Preservation vacuum pumps are utilized for a wide range of industrial and commercial applications. The market types include diaphragm pumps, piston pumps, liquid ring pumps, and other varieties. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create a vacuum, while piston pumps use a piston to displace air. Liquid ring pumps use a liquid to create the vacuum and are commonly used in more demanding applications. Other market types may include rotary vane pumps, scroll pumps, and turbomolecular pumps, each offering unique advantages for specific applications.</p></p>
