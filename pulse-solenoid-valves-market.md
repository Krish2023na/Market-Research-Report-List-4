<p><p>Pulse Solenoid Valves are classified into two main types based on their design and application: Embedded Pulse Solenoid Valves and Straight Through Pulse Solenoid Valves. Embedded Pulse Solenoid Valves are compact and feature a direct connection to the fluid flow path, making them ideal for applications where space is limited. On the other hand, Straight Through Pulse Solenoid Valves have a linear flow path, allowing for high flow rates and minimal pressure drop, making them suitable for applications requiring efficient fluid control.</p></p>
