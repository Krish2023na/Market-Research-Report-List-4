<p><p>In the Railway Wheel-Sets market, there are two main types based on the arrangement of bearings: Outboard Bearings Arrangement and Inboard Bearing Arrangement. In the Outboard Bearings Arrangement, the bearings are located outside the wheels, providing stability and support. On the other hand, the Inboard Bearing Arrangement has the bearings placed inside the wheels, allowing for a more compact design. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different applications within the railway industry.</p></p>
