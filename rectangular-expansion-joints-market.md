<p><p>Rectangular expansion joints are used to absorb movements in buildings caused by temperature changes. These joints come in various types including fillet type, single oblique angle, and camera angle. Fillet type joints are used in areas where minimal movement is expected, single oblique angle joints are used when there is movement in one direction, and camera angle joints are used when there is movement in two directions. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements in construction projects.</p></p>
