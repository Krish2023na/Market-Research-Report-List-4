<p><ul><li>Surface Safety Valves</li><li>Subsurface Safety Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Safety valves are crucial components in the oil and gas industry to prevent accidents and ensure operational safety. Surface safety valves are installed at the wellhead and serve as the primary safety mechanism for controlling pressure during production. On the other hand, subsurface safety valves are located downhole and are used to shut off the flow of fluids in case of emergencies. Both types of safety valves play a vital role in maintaining safe and efficient operations in the oil and gas sector.</p></p>
