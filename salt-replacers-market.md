<p><p>Salt replacers are alternatives to traditional salt used in food products to reduce sodium content. They come in three main types: liquid, powder, and crystals. Liquid salt replacers are convenient for use in liquid-based products like soups and sauces. Powdered salt replacers are versatile and can be easily incorporated into dry mixes and seasoning blends. Crystalline salt replacers are similar in appearance to traditional salt and can be used as a direct substitute in applications like baking. Each type offers a different form for manufacturers to use based on their specific needs.</p></p>
