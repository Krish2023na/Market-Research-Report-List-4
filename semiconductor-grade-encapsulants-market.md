<p><p>Semiconductor grade encapsulants are used in the electronics industry to protect and insulate sensitive electronic components. There are two main types of encapsulants in the market: one-component and two-component. One-component encapsulants are pre-mixed and ready to use, offering convenience and ease of application. Both types of encapsulants play a crucial role in safeguarding electronic devices against environmental factors and mechanical stress.</p></p>
