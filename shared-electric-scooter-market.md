<p><ul><li>Dockless</li><li>Station-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dockless shared electric scooter systems allow users to rent scooters on-demand and drop them off at their destination, without needing to return them to a specific location. Users locate and unlock scooters using a mobile app. On the other hand, station-based shared electric scooter systems require users to pick up and drop off scooters at designated locations, or stations, throughout the city. Users often need to pre-book scooters at specific stations and return them to the same station after use. Both types offer convenient and sustainable transportation options for urban dwellers.</p></p>
