<p><strong>The Smart Natural Gas Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMR</li><li>AMI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Natural Gas meters can be categorized into two main types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR technology allows gas consumption data to be collected remotely and transmitted to the utility company for billing purposes. On the other hand, AMI systems enable two-way communication between the meter and the utility company, providing real-time data on gas usage and allowing for more efficient monitoring and management. Both technologies play a crucial role in modernizing the natural gas industry.</p></p>
