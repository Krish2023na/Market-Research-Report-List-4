<p><p>Specialized examination tables come in three main types - electric, manual, and hydraulic. Electric examination tables are powered and controlled by a motor, offering smooth and precise adjustments. Manual examination tables are operated manually through levers or knobs, providing basic functionality at a lower cost. Hydraulic examination tables use hydraulic systems for height adjustments, providing a balance between cost and convenience. Each type caters to different preferences and budgets, catering to the diverse needs of medical professionals and their patients.</p></p>
