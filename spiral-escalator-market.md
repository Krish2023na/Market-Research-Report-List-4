<p><ul><li>Isokinetic Operation</li><li>Frequency Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spiral escalators use isokinetic operation and frequency conversion technology in their design. Isokinetic operation ensures a smooth and even movement of the escalator steps, reducing wear and tear on the machinery. Frequency conversion allows for the speed of the escalator to be adjusted according to traffic flow, saving energy and reducing maintenance costs. Both technologies improve the overall efficiency and performance of spiral escalators, making them a popular choice in modern buildings.</p></p>
