Composite Membranes consist of multiple layers with varying properties, providing high salt rejection and durability. Asymmetric Membranes have a dense active layer on one side and a porous support layer on the other, offering high flux rates and resistance to fouling. Both types play a crucial role in water desalination, wastewater treatment, and other filtration processes, catering to diverse needs in various industries.</p></p>
