<p><p>Static Var Compensators (SVCs) are devices used in power systems to regulate voltage and reactive power. One type of SVC is Thyristor-Based, which uses thyristors to control the flow of reactive power. Another type is Magnetically Controlled Reactor (MCR)-Based, which uses magnetic fields to control reactive power flow. Both types of SVCs play a crucial role in ensuring the stability and efficiency of power systems by dynamically adjusting reactive power to maintain voltage levels within desired limits.</p></p>
