<p><p>Stationary battery testing equipment market consists of three main types: cell testing, module testing, and pack testing. Cell testing involves evaluating individual battery cells to measure their performance and capacity. Module testing focuses on testing groups of cells that are connected together within a battery system. Pack testing involves testing the entire battery pack to ensure optimal performance and safety. Each type of testing equipment is crucial for assessing the overall health and functionality of stationary batteries used in various applications.</p></p>
