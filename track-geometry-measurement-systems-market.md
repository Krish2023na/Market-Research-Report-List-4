<p><p>Track Geometry Measurement Systems refer to technologies used in the railway industry to assess the condition of tracks and ensure safe train operations. There are three main types of systems in the market - Track Geometry Trolley, Track Geometry Inspection Vehicle (TGIV), and Autonomous Track Geometry Measurement System (ATGMS). Track Geometry Trolleys are manually pushed along tracks to collect data, TGIVs are vehicles equipped with sensors for automated data collection, and ATGMS are unmanned systems that utilize advanced technologies like LiDAR and GPS for track inspection.</p></p>
