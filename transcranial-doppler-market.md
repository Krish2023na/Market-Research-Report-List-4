<p><strong>The Transcranial Doppler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>M-mode Display</li><li>B-mode Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transcranial Doppler market includes two main types: M-mode Display and B-mode Display. M-mode Display provides a one-dimensional display of blood flow velocities within the brain, focusing on the movement of blood over time. B-mode Display, on the other hand, offers a two-dimensional image of blood flow within the brain, allowing for a more detailed visualization of blood vessels and potential abnormalities. Both types of displays are used in clinical settings for assessing blood flow in the brain and diagnosing conditions such as stroke and cerebral vasospasm.</p></p>
