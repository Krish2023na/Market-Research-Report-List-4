<p><p>Two Wheeler Lead Acid Batteries market consists of two main types: VRLA Batteries and Flooded Batteries. VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) Batteries are maintenance-free, sealed, and suitable for applications where minimal maintenance and ventilation are desired. Flooded Batteries require regular maintenance, including adding water to the cells, and are commonly used in applications where ventilation is not a concern. Both types have their unique advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
