<p><ul><li>Single Common Anode</li><li>Dual Common Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrafast rectifier diodes come in two main market types: Single Common Anode and Dual Common Anode. In Single Common Anode diodes, there is one shared anode terminal for all the diodes within a package, simplifying the circuit layout and reducing component count. On the other hand, Dual Common Anode diodes have two shared anode terminals, allowing for even higher efficiency and faster switching speeds. Both types offer high performance and reliability in various power electronics applications.</p></p>
