<p><ul><li>Hydronic Heating</li><li>Electric Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfloor heating market types include Hydronic Heating and Electric Heating. Hydronic heating systems use water to heat the floor, making them efficient and cost-effective. They are ideal for larger spaces and can be powered by various energy sources. On the other hand, Electric heating systems use electric cables or mats to generate heat, providing quick and responsive warmth. They are easy to install and are suitable for smaller areas. Both types offer comfortable and energy-efficient heating solutions for residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>
