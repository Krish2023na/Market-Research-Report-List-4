<p><p>Universal centrifuges are categorized into various market types based on the type of rotor they use. Swing rotor centrifuges are designed to accommodate a range of tube sizes and shapes by swinging out horizontally during operation. Fixed angle rotor centrifuges have tubes that remain at a fixed angle during operation, allowing for better separation of samples. Other types of centrifuges in the market include microcentrifuges, refrigerated centrifuges, and high-speed centrifuges, each designed for specific applications and sample sizes.</p></p>
