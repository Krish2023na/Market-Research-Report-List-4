<p><p>Wheelchair lifting platforms are designed to help individuals in wheelchairs navigate different levels in a building. Two common types in the market are vertical platform lifts and incline platform lifts. Vertical platform lifts move straight up and down, providing a vertical transport solution for individuals with mobility challenges. Incline platform lifts are designed to navigate stairs and slopes, allowing wheelchair users to seamlessly access different levels in a building. Both types offer safe and reliable alternatives to traditional methods of vertical transportation.</p></p>
